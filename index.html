About Melbourne Websites

Founded in 2015, Melbourne Websites began with a simple mission: to provide local businesses with world-class web design solutions at accessible prices. What started as a small team of passionate developers has grown into Melbourne's most trusted web design agency, serving over 500 satisfied clients across various industries.

Our core values of innovation, transparency, and client success drive everything we do. We believe in creating websites that not only look stunning but also deliver measurable business results. Our team of certified developers, designers, and digital strategists works tirelessly to stay ahead of industry trends and technological advancements, ensuring your website remains at the cutting edge of digital excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical website development timeline?

Most projects are completed within 4-6 weeks, following our proven 5-phase development process. Complex e-commerce sites may take 8-10 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during initial consultation.

What's included in your hosting package?

Our premium hosting includes 99.9% uptime guarantee, daily backups, SSL certificates, CDN integration, 24/7 monitoring, and regular security updates. We also provide monthly performance reports.

Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages including regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support. Choose from monthly, quarterly, or annual plans.

How do you handle website security?

We implement enterprise-level security measures including SSL encryption, regular malware scanning, firewall protection, and automated backup systems. All sites receive monthly security audits.

What's your pricing structure?

Projects start from $999 for basic sites and scale based on functionality requirements. We offer flexible payment plans and provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs.
